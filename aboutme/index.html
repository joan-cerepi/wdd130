About Me

Hello everyone! I am from the city of Korçë, Albania. If you have never heard of it, Albania is a small country in Europe located between Greece and Italy. I have a younger sister and I am currently years old.

I am a convert to the church. I was baptized and confirmed a member of the church in December 2016. My strong love and testimony of the gospel gave me the desire to serve a mission. I was called to the Bulgaria/Central Eurasian Mission, Turkish speaking and served in the country of Bulgaria.

I am currently a student in the Applied Technology program of BYU-Pathway. My hobbies include reading, teaching, playing video games, horseback riding, doing math, writing code, ethical hacking (penetration testing) etc. I have been into computers since I was a kid and the last few years I have been teaching myself how to do ethical hacking. My dream job is to become a web app & network penetration tester. Finding vulnerabilities and security bugs in code feels like a game to me and I love the idea of helping make the Internet a safer place. Some of the coding languages I have had exposure to are: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Php, SQL, Go, C++, Java.
